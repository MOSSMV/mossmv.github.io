MOSS Logo

A strong image is a consistent image That’s why we’ve developed MOSS logo usage guidelines to help ensure the world sees the same MOSS in everything we do. Please find below a top-level summary of the MOSS logo usage guideline, for more please download the complete MOSS Logo usage guidelines..

  1. Only use approved artwork of the MOSS logo. Never recreate the logo with your own fonts.
  2. When resizing the logo keep the height and length in the proper proportion.
  3. It should only appear in the colors Black and PMS 281c.
